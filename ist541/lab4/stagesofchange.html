Stages of Change

1. Precontemplation

Not yet acknowledging a problem; low/no intent to change.

2. Contemplation

Ambivalence; weighing pros and cons; “maybe someday.”

3. Preparation

Intention to act; making small steps and gathering resources.

4. Action

Visible behavior change within the last 6 months.

5. Maintenance

Sustaining change; preventing relapse and building routines.

6. Relapse / Recycling

Returning to prior behavior; reflect, learn, and re-engage.
