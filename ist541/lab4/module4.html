MODULE 4:

GOAL SETTING

"Now What?"

It all comes back to the goal! Creating a goal in therapy is an art, it is a fluid mix of structure and flexibility. And when things get tough, your colleagues will always remind you, "what is your goal?" A goal is the center of the work, where the beginning phases lead to and where we strive to get to. Make it collaborative with the client!
