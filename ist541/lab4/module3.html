MOTIVATIONAL INTERVIEWING

"What Do I Say?"

Start here for a high-level orientation. Then open the module you need from the menu above.

Four Phases of Motivational Interviewing

1. Engaging

Not yet acknowledging a problem; low/no intent to change.

2. Evoking

Ambivalence; weighing pros and cons; “maybe someday.”

3. Focusing

Intention to act; making small steps and gathering resources.

4. Planning

Visible behavior change within the last 6 months.
