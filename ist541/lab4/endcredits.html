The People

Appreciation goes out to the Professors, Advisors, and Staff of the California State University, Monterey Bay, the School of Computing & Design, and the MIST Program. With clearly designed curriculum, they shared their expertise in a fun and supportive learning environment! The Program

The Master of Science in Instructional Science and Technology program at CSUMB is an interdisciplinary program that integrates information technology, instructional design, and education technology. Skills developed from the program: how to better use 21st century learning technologies; K-12 and Higher Education learning theory; educational assessment and evaluation; instructional design for corporate training; research methods for scholarly publication. School of Computing and Design, the MIST program.
